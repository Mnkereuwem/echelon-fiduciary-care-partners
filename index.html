Professional Capabilities & Documentation

Court-ready evidence, forensic risk metrics, and clinical asset protection frameworks.

1

Incapacity & Trust Vulnerability (Pre-Litigation Protection)

Independent Functional Capacity Assessment

A highly objective, formal clinical evaluation mapping the elder's cognitive functioning directly to their real-world environment. Documents precisely how the client understands their assets, who their heirs are, and the exact intent behind their decisions—bound directly to the trust execution file as insurance against future "lack of capacity" lawsuits.

Fluctuation & Sun-Downing Log

For clients with early-to-mid stage dementia who experience fluctuating capacity (lucid in the morning, confused by 4:00 PM). Generates an objective, multi-day clinical log establishing the precise window of day where the client exhibits maximum cognitive clarity, giving the attorney a legally defensible "safe window" to execute documents.

Undue Influence Risk Audit

Utilizing the California KAMER Method (California Probate Code § 15610.70) to evaluate vulnerability, authority, tactics, and inequitable results. Delivers a forensic clinical report detailing whether a suspicious live-in caregiver or overbearing family member is actively manipulating the elder's isolated environment.

2

Active Trust Litigation & Conservatorship (Court Evidence)

Psychosocial Assessment for CAC

A thorough evaluation requested by or built for Court Appointed Counsel to establish the elder's true "Least Restrictive Environment." Provides the probate judge with an unbiased, clinical blueprint of what the elder actually needs, stripping away the emotional exaggerations of competing beneficiaries.

Care Management & Exploitation Timelines

In cases of alleged financial or physical elder abuse, we audit medical charts, facility incident reports, and care logs. Builds a chronological, clinical timeline detailing exactly when necessary care was withheld or when cognitive decline was exploited for financial extraction.

Professional Care Declarations

Formally structured, signed court declarations summarizing clinical findings, diagnostic indicators, and professional placement trajectories that litigation attorneys drop directly into their active motions.

3

End-of-Life & Acute Crisis Logistics (Trustee Indemnification)

Care & Placement Justification Matrix

A formal framework detailing exactly why an elder requires high-end, secured memory care over standard assisted living. By detailing acute behavioral, medical, and wandering risks, we give the Corporate Trustee bulletproof legal justification to release trust funds without fear of beneficiary backlash.

Bespoke Crisis Care Plans

A comprehensive clinical blueprint mapping seamless transitions from acute hospital environments (such as Kaiser) or intensive ICU step-downs directly into premium private-duty home care or specialized hospice networks.

Placement Intake & Discharge Summaries

Contemporaneous, highly objective clinical logs covering every luxury facility toured, every medical director vetted, and the deterministic variables behind placement decisions tailored strictly to client acuity.

4

Advance Care Planning & Medical Autonomy (The Estate Portfolio)

Advance Health Care Directive Reports

While legal teams draft the instrument, we manage the intensive clinical discovery. We deliver a narrative report mapping structural choices (ventilators, artificial nutrition) directly to personal values, proving zero duress during execution.

POLST Alignment Mapping

Translating abstract legal directives into explicit, actionable Physician Orders for Life-Sustaining Treatment (POLST) that regional emergency first responders and emergency departments must legally execute.

HIPAA & Trigger Clause Clearances

Compiling and managing secure physician certifications confirming exactly when a trust's structural "incapacity clause" has been triggered, allowing a successor co-trustee to assume immediate operational authority without probate intervention.
